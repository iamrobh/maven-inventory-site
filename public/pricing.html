Frequently asked questions

What counts as an integration?

One connection to an external platform counts as one integration. Amazon US (including both FBA and FBM) counts as 1 integration. Each Shopify store is 1 integration. QuickBooks or Xero each count as 1 integration.

Do you charge per user or per order?

No. All plans include unlimited users, unlimited products, and unlimited orders. Your price is based on your plan tier and number of integrations.

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to your selected plan for 30 days. Cancel anytime. Connect your channels, import your data, and see the full picture before you commit.

What's the difference between standard and AI-powered forecasting?

Standard forecasting uses moving and seasonal averages to generate reorder and transfer recommendations. AI-powered forecasting adds machine learning models with advanced seasonality detection and trend analysis for more accurate demand predictions.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
